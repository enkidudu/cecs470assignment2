Undergraduation - B.I.E.T (2008 - 2012)

To pursue my career in "Computer Science", I had to take numerous steps towards my goal of becoming an engineer in Computer Science from one of the renowned institutions in India. To further equip myself with intellectual depth & breadth along the greater practical knowledge which, I knew that a bachelor’s degree in Computer Science is what I wanted to pursue after graduating from high school. Graduating from this university, opened a much wider perspective of various opportunities in Computer Science. Faculty staff at my college played a key role in developing a strong skill set in the field of Computer Science. Early on in college, I was introduced to programming languages including C, C++, JAVA. Also, I became familiar with UNIX and Linux platforms. I then went on to take courses such as Computer organization, Computer Architecture, Software Engineering, Data Structures, Computer Networks and Artificial Intelligence to help deepen my theoretical understanding in Computer Science. In addition to the courses in Computer science, I was also given a brief overview to other core subjects of the major fields in Engineering.

During my Sophomore year, my professor who was also the Dean of the University, taught C++ which gave me a strong fundamental skill towards coding applications in the same year. Then, I chose Data Base Management Systems (DBMS), which turned out to be my most exciting subject. I was introduced to different database models such as the hierarchical model, network model, the relational model, multi-dimensional model, object relational model, object oriented model. I then went on to take additional courses in DBMS including Relational DBMS (RDBMS), Object Oriented Databases and XML Databases. I also started working on various database tools including Oracle 8I, SQL Server, MySQL etc., that were used in developing dynamic web applications.

It was in my Junior year that I worked on my first real world project, "BARCODE GENERATOR & RECOGNIZER", the process of generating and reading barcodes without using any of the hardware generating machines. This project in particular has shaped me into becoming an innovative engineer to design solutions for modern day challenges. The technologies that I have learnt played an important role in developing many other projects. In addition to developing applications, I was left with an ardent desire to pursue a career in research.

In my Senior year, with a deepest interest in database systems and application development, I wanted to develop a project that could give a great deal of advantage not only to the end-users of the technology but also help our deteriorating environment. Hence I developed my own project called "ECO-CYCLE", a web-based application that recommends users, the different ways of reusing/recycling products in every day life. This application is now maintained by my University which is open to all the students in the University. The user interface provides different methods we could use to recycle our everyday products in effective manner and also to sell them at a lower price. Application is also designed to provide important techniques to recycle some of the harmful products like non-biodegradable plastics. In developing this application, I employed PHP and HTML with JQuery along with MySQL, as these technologies are still open source and also are easy to maintain. I am currently writing applications using PHP with several database tools that can generate dynamic webpages.
